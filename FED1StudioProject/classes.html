Class Subjects

Tax

In this class we will teach you how to learn how to prepare individual income tax returns, we will teach you how to complete business tax returns, learn how to tax plan, conduct 1099 processing, and conduct estimated tax payments.

Auditing

Finacial statement audits. Employee benefit plans. Footnote disclosures. Make recommadations to client management

Budgeting/forecasting

Prepare 3 year budgeted finicial statements. Prepare quarterly forecasting. Update previous forecasts. Prepare new forecasts.

Account Payable/Receivable

Prepare purchase orders. Enter and pay invoices. Keep a detailed vendor information. Maintain customer lists. Enter cash reciepts.

Fixed Assets

Record all new fixed assets. A fixed asset is something that has to be depriciated. Record depriciation expense. Perform periodic fixed asset inventories.

Cash Managment

Reconcile bank accounts. Estimate future cash needs. Establish banking relationships.
