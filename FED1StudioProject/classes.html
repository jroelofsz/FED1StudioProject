Class Subjects

Tax

In this class we will teach you how to prepare individual income tax returns, complete business tax returns, learn how to tax plan, conduct 1099 processing, and conduct estimated tax payments.

Auditing

This class will teach you the skills of how to do finacial statement audits, employee benefit plans, footnote disclosures, and how to make profressional recommandations to client management

Budgeting/forecasting

This class will teach you how to prepare 3 year budgeted finacial statements, prepare quarterly forecasting, and how to update those forecasts.

Account Payable/Receivable

This class will teach you how to prepare purchase orders, enter and pay invoices, keep a detailed vendor information, maintain customer lists, enter cash reciepts.

Fixed Assets

In this course you will learn how to record all new fixed assets, a fixed asset is something that has to be depriciated, record depriciation expense, and perform periodic fixed asset inventories.

Cash Managment

This course will teach you how to reconcile bank accounts, estimate future cash needs, and establish banking relationships.
